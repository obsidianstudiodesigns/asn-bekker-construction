03 — How we build

From slab to
snag list.

Every project runs through the same four stages. Scroll to watch it come together.

  1. 01

    Site visit & quote

    We come out, measure up and listen to what you actually want. You get a written, itemised quote — free, and with no obligation.

  2. 02

    Foundations & structure

    Slab, brickwork and structural steel set out square and level. Get this stage right and everything after it is easier.
